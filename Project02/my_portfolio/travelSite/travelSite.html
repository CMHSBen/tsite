Where to Stay

Florida is a great place, and to enjoy it fully for an extended period of time, you might have to book a hotel. Here are some good reccomendations by a trusted site.

U.S. News and World Report ranks the best hotels in Florida based on an analysis of industry awards, hotel star ratings and user ratings. Hotels that scored in the top 10% of the Best Hotels in USA earned a Gold badge. Hotels that scored in the top 30% of the Best Hotels in Florida earned a Silver badge. Hotels that appear after ranked hotels are sorted by hotel class and then by user rating, as provided by Tripadvisor.
